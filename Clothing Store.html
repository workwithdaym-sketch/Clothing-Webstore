Frequently Asked
When will my order arrive?+
Standard shipping takes 3–5 business days. Express shipping (1–2 days) is available at checkout. You'll receive a tracking number once your order ships.
How do I return an item?+
You have 30 days from delivery to return unworn items. Visit the Returns page, enter your order number, and print your free return label. Refunds process in 5–7 business days.
What sizes do you carry?+
We carry sizes XS through 3XL for clothing and US 4–16 for footwear. Extended sizes are available on select styles. Check the size guide on each product page.
Do you offer student discounts?+
Yes! Nike members with a verified student status receive 10% off full-price items. Sign up for Nike membership and verify your student status through UNiDAYS.
Are Nike products authentic on your site?+
100%. Every product on Nike.com is authentic, sourced directly from Nike factories. We never sell third-party or grey-market goods.
